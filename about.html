About Me

Howdy, I'm Sierra Stephenson — a web designer and front-end developer based in Henderson, Texas, creating digital experiences that are intentionally simple and unapologetically bold. My journey into tech began in high school when I worked with my school district's tech department. Troubleshooting, problem-solving, and seeing how technology could transform everyday operations — that's when I knew this was my path. I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Phoenix (graduated August 2025) and specialize in building clean, functional interfaces that balance user needs with business goals. I believe the best work happens when creativity meets structured problem-solving, and that every project deserves both strategic thinking and thoughtful design.

What drives me: Representation matters. I got into tech because I saw what was possible — and I stayed because I realized how few people who look like me get to build it. I'm committed to creating work that proves you belong here, no matter your background.

Beyond the Work

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me exploring 3D modeling in Blender, reading, or playing The Sims 4 and inZOI. I'm always learning and growing, both as a developer and designer.
